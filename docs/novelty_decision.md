# Novelty Decision

Decision after v2 hardening: workshop-only.

Reason: The failure-composition framing is useful, but the current evidence is synthetic and the original interaction-aware predictor is exact because it matches the data-generating law. V2 hardening shows that omitted higher-order interactions can break a pairwise composition law.

## Surviving Contribution

- Shift robustness reporting from isolated sensor failures to composed-failure laws.
- Show that additive assumptions are testable and can fail.
- Require reports to state interaction order and stress omitted common-cause cells.

## Novelty Boundary

The paper is a mechanism and evaluation-protocol note. It is not yet a real-robot robustness result, a complete theory of sensor failure composition, or evidence that pairwise interactions are enough.
