# Submission Readiness Decision

Decision: workshop-only.

Rationale: The paper has a useful mechanism framing for sensor-failure composition, but v2 hardening shows low-order laws fail under omitted higher-order/common-cause failures. The strongest v2 failure is held-out triple-cell pairwise MAE 0.758.

Allowed framing: workshop-level mechanism and reporting protocol for composed sensor failures.

Disallowed framing: real-robot robustness, universal pairwise composition, or algorithmic superiority from the matched synthetic law.
