# Novelty Decision

Chosen thesis: sensor-failure effects are compositional, not additive, and robustness should be modeled as an interaction law over failure factors.

Why this survives hostile priors: benchmark-only, bigger-model, uncertainty, and verifier-style fixes all leave the interaction law unchanged.
Central mechanism: estimate and test low-dimensional interaction structure over sensor failures, then demonstrate that single-failure robustness mispredicts composed failures.
